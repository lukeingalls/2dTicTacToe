How To Play

×

The box you choose determines where your opponent must play. A move in the small board corresponds to the board your opponent will have to play in on the big board. The gif below shows this where the cursor hovers on a potential move and the green highlight shows where the opponent would have to go.

Be careful! Sending your opponent to an occupied tile will let them choose where they can go!

And of course 3 in a row across the big board is a win!

Enjoy!
